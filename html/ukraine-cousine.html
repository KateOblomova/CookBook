Ukrainian Cuisine

The unique characteristics of Ukrainian cuisine have developed over many centuries due to various factors such as neighbouring countries, climate, fertile soil, and diligent locals. Ukrainian dishes are known for their rich flavours, often seasoned with garlic, and despite the use of contrasting tastes, they blend together seamlessly.
