Brazilian Cuisine

Brazilian cuisine is very regionalized, each region has its own typical dishes. This is the result of a mixture of different European, Indigenous, and African ingredients and influences.

Classic Brigadeiros

Ingridients

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 14 oz sweetened condensed milk(395 g)
  • Â¼ cup cocoa powder(30 g)
  • 1 cup chocolate sprinkle(160 g), as needed

Directions

In a pot over low heat, melt the butter, condensed milk, and cocoa powder, stirring continuously until you can see the bottom of the pot for 2-3 seconds when dragging a spatula through. Pour onto a greased plate, then chill for 1 hour. Shape and roll the chilled mixture into balls. Roll the balls in chocolate sprinkles. Enjoy!
